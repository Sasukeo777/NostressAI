title: "Digital Detox: The 20-20-20 Rule"
excerpt: "Reduce eye strain and mental fatigue by looking away every 20 minutes."
date: "2024-01-15"

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This simple habit resets your visual system and gives your brain a micro-break.

## Why a Framework?

Most stress advice is scattered: "sleep more," "meditate," "batch your tasks." But without a **mental model** connecting these tactics, you end up with a patchwork of habits that don't reinforce each other.

The 9 Pillars are different. They give you a structured lens to diagnose **where** stress is leaking in‚Äîand a clear map for which interventions to layer.

## How to Use This Framework

1. **Identify Friction**: Which pillar is causing the loudest friction today?
2. **Layer One Habit**: Start with a single micro-habit in that pillar.
3. **Cross-Pollinate**: Once stable, add a complementary habit from another meta-category.

For example: If **Sleep** is your bottleneck, start with an evening wind-down ritual. Once that's stable, layer in a **Work** protocol like async-first communication to protect your mornings.
